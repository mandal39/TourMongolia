Overview

The tour takes place on the Onon River basin in the Khentii province, east of Mongolia, with 4 days fishing in the wild and pristine nature. In addition to the natural features, it is possible to get an unforgettable memory of historical sites and beautiful mountains, river, canyons and unique places nearby Mongolian-Russian border. There are some fishing tools available, and a local guide and a fishing guide can help to make your tour wonderful. Fishing license and permission to enter in border region is obtained in accordance with Mongolian law and the preparation for the trip is well done. Note that most of the trip is through swampy dirt roads in the forest-steppe zone.
